# Gamification System

Version: 1.0

Status: Core Experience

---

# Philosophy

Gamification should reinforce learning.

It should never encourage meaningless engagement.

Students earn rewards for remembering, improving, and staying consistent.

---

# Core Rewards

XP

Earned for:

- Completing study sessions.
- Recovering forgotten concepts.
- Solving difficult questions.
- Maintaining revision streaks.

---

Levels

Represent overall learning progression.

Levels unlock:

- Themes
- Achievements
- Milestones

---

Badges

Examples:

Memory Guardian

Recovered 100 forgotten concepts.

---

Consistency Champion

30-day study streak.

---

Concept Master

100% confidence in a chapter.

---

PYQ Slayer

Solved 100 previous-year questions.

---

Memory Rescuer

Recovered concepts with low confidence.

---

# Daily Missions

Examples:

Review one weak topic.

Recover one forgotten concept.

Solve five adaptive questions.

Maintain today's study streak.

---

# Weekly Challenges

Examples:

Improve Retention Index.

Complete all scheduled revisions.

Master one new chapter.

---

# Streaks

Study Streak

Consecutive study days.

---

Revision Streak

Consecutive days completing scheduled revisions.

---

Recovery Streak

Consecutive days recovering forgotten concepts.

---

# Progress Visualization

Dashboard shows:

- Retention Index
- Knowledge Points
- Scholar Rank
- Retention Index
- Streaks
- Achievements
- Weekly Progress

---

# Motivation Agent

Responsible for:

- Celebrating milestones.
- Sending reminders.
- Detecting burnout.
- Encouraging breaks.
- Maintaining healthy motivation.

---

# Guiding Rule

Reward learning outcomes, not screen time.
